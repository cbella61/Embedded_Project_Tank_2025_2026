# Cannon module with electromagnet

## Confirmed functional requirements

- The PCB with the electromagnet, its mechanism and the barrel must be
  embedded in the moving part of the turret.
- This entire assembly must rotate together with the cradle driven by the two
  servos.
- Existing holes on the PCB must be used for attachment; do not invent a
  different drilling pattern.
- The stepper still rotates the entire turret in the XY plane.
- The 50 x 50 x 2 mm PCB of the firing module is part of the moving group.

## Confirmed dimensions

- base material/type: PCB, not a metal plate;
- PCB: 50 x 50 x 2 mm;
- diameter of the four holes: 3.5 mm;
- center of each hole 5 mm from the two adjacent PCB edges; coordinates from
  the PCB center are therefore X = ±20 mm and Y = ±20 mm;
- complete cannon in the solenoid area: maximum external diameter 20 mm;
- cylindrical/tubular barrel: external diameter 10 mm and wall thickness 1 mm,
  therefore internal diameter 8 mm;
- barrel protrusion from the cannon exit: 46 mm;
- total height from the PCB bottom face to the top of the assembly: 23 mm.


- lateral envelope of the electromagnet body: about 25 mm;
- width shown in the top view: about 30 mm;

These last values must not be used yet to generate geometry.

## Assembly choice to verify on prototype

The PCB will be centered on the moving cradle and fastened using the four
confirmed holes. The final position of the elevation axis relative to the
module can be adjusted in the parameters after verifying the real balance.
